Kebab

Ingredients


  • 500g ground lamb or beef
  • 1 small onion ,chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic ,minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • Â½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsely
  • Skewers (if using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes before using)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method


  • Step 1

    In a large bowl, mix together the ground meat, chopped onion, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, coriander, cinnamon, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley or cilantro. Use your hands to mix everything together until well combined.

  • Step 2

    Divide the meat mixture into equal portions and shape each portion into a long sausage-like shape around the skewers. Press the meat firmly onto the skewers to prevent them from falling off during cooking.

  • Step 3

    Preheat your grill or barbecue to medium-high heat. If you're using an indoor grill or oven, preheat it to the highest temperature setting.

  • Step 4

    Brush the kebabs with olive oil on all sides. This will help them cook evenly and prevent them from sticking to the grill.

  • Step 5

    Place the kebabs on the preheated grill and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and nicely browned on all sides. Make sure to rotate the kebabs to ensure even cooking.

  • Step 6

    Once cooked, remove the kebabs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.Serve the kebabs hot with your favorite accompaniments, such as bread, rice and salad.
